RidRally:Throw Away, Give Away, Sell Away

Ready to start the month with a goal to get rid of stuff? Whether you're single, married, or bringing the whole family along for the ride, this app helps you stay accountable and make real progress toward getting rid of what you don't need—and creating momentum for positive change in your life. Let’s do this! 💪

Throw away: Aim for this to be your main source of riddance.

Give away: This is your go-to for second chances: thrift stores, friends, or coworkers.

Sell away: Let’s be honest — how often do we say, ‘I’m just trying to sell this,’ but never really follow through? Don’t make this your last resort: if you can’t sell it, give it away or throw it out!
